Our Story

Three Generations of Excellence

From a small family workshop in Denizli to a global textile manufacturer, our journey is woven with passion, quality, and Turkish craftsmanship.

1998

Founded in Denizli

Started as a family workshop in Turkey's textile heartland, carrying forward generations of weaving tradition.

2005

Oeko-Tex Certified

Achieved our first international certification, proving our commitment to safe, sustainable textiles.

2010

Global Expansion

About Us

Trusted Textile Partner
Since 1998

Cotton Textile is a leading Turkish textile manufacturer based in Denizli, Turkey's textile heartland. For over 25 years, we've been the trusted B2B partner for hotels, spas, retailers, and hospitality brands worldwide.

Our commitment to quality is backed by international certifications including Oeko-Tex Standard 100 and ISO 9001:2015. Every product is crafted using 100% organic Aegean cotton with traditional hand-weaving techniques.
